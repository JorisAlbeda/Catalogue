# The Accord Hall

## Description

The Accord Hall was constructed from reinforced ashwood and stone taken from the ruins of the Ruined Temple. It stands as a physical representation of unity and reconciliation among its builders. The structure's design symbolizes the possibility of mending even the most fractured alliances through shared purpose and understanding.

## History

The Guild established The Accord Hall as a symbol of their collective resolve to resolve past conflicts. It was built with the intention of fostering dialogue among those who had once been divided. The hall's creation marked a significant step toward reconciliation and the pursuit of common goals.

## Location

Charming Valley
