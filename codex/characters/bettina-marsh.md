# Bettina Marsh

## Description

Bettina Marsh was an old friend of Halvard Cray. She remained at the mill door throughout two wars, never leaving her post. She kept Halvard Cray's iron key on the same nail since before Perrin was born, never once being asked to remove it. Her silence and steadfast presence made her a figure of quiet authority in Fertile Valley. She observed the conflict without direct involvement. Her role as a silent guardian extended to those who sought her counsel, though she spoke little. The key she guarded was both a symbol of past oaths and a tool for unlocking futures yet to unfold.

## History

The name Bettina Marsh shows up in many old letters by Halvard Cray. Some old diary entries suggest Perrin Ashe consulted her as well when founding Ashvale, but considering her old age during Halvard's war, this has been dismissed as a misinterpretation, although it has been posited that there were in fact two Bettina Marshes, one who lived during Halvard's rule and one who lived to see Ashvale's beginnings.
