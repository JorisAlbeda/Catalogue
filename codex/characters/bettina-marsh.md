# Bettina Marsh

## Description
Bettina Marsh remained at the mill door throughout the war, never leaving her post. She kept Halvard Cray's iron key on the same nail since before Perrin was born, never once being asked to remove it. Her silence and steadfast presence made her a figure of quiet authority in Fertile Valley. She observed the conflict without direct involvement, her actions shaped by the weight of history and unspoken promises. Her role as a silent guardian extended to those who sought her counsel, though she spoke little. The key she guarded was both a symbol of past oaths and a tool for unlocking futures yet to unfold.

## History
Bettina Marsh was present at Fertile Valley from the war's beginning, arriving before any others. She witnessed the Chancellor's attempts to claim the valley and the declaration of Halvard Cray's war, which she did not abandon. When the Chancellor offered citizenship, rebuilt lands, and a relic from her father's past, she declined, choosing loyalty to the valley over imperial rewards. She learned the Empire's name for Daz, the Bandit Witch of the Narrow Pass, during a revelation that tied her past to the war's present. Her quiet resolve shaped the valley's fate, as she upheld oaths made long before her time. The key she protected became a silent testament to her role in the conflict's unfolding.
