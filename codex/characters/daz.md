# Daz

## Description
Daz was known as the Bandit Witch of the Narrow Pass, a title the Empire used to label her as an enemy of the Chancellor. She was a Bandit Chief who first swore loyalty to the Empire, though she spoke as if she had lived many lives. Her abilities included discarding Hearth and Order cards at sites in her region when played. She was a strategist who gathered remnants of her followers after losing ground to the Chancellor's forces. Daz was described as stubborn, with a quiet resolve that refused to yield to the Chancellor's demands. Her presence in the Narrow Pass was marked by a defiance that made her both feared and respected by those who crossed her path.

## History
Daz was the first Bandit Chief to pledge allegiance to the Empire, yet she remained an enigmatic figure who challenged the Chancellor's authority. She lost followers and territory to the Chancellor's campaigns, forcing her to retreat to the Narrow Pass and the Hinterland. After years of struggling to reclaim her former status, she settled into a life of exile, gathering what little remained of her people. She interacted with figures like Ossa, Fen Quill, and Marlow Ash, who accompanied her during confrontations with the Chancellor. Daz's leadership was tested when she sent two of her own people to escort Sorrel Wick, acknowledging her as an ally rather than a subject. Her defiance of the Chancellor's demands solidified her reputation as a Bandit Witch, a name the Empire openly used to brand her as an enemy.
