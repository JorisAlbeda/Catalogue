# Halvard Cray

## Description
Halvard Cray was the sitting Chancellor in the 200th Year of the Oak. Controlling and meticulous, he sought a clean, orderly reign, but his need to check everything himself blinded him to betrayal brewing close by.

## History
Halvard Cray swore the Oath of Supremacy, and reigned in the Fertile Valley until the Sorcerer of the Woods overthrew his regime.

## Location
Ruined Temple

## Status
Deceased
