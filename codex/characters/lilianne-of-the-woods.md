# Lilianne of the Woods

## Description
Lilianne of the Woods was a fiery and passionate leader with sharp blue eyes and black hair, wrapped in a mantel that seemed too large for her. She was driven by a strong sense of justice and a determination to fill her father's role. Her relationships were complex, often involving heated discussions with Sorrel, yet she valued Sorrel's advice in critical moments. She trusted Naiad as both a mother and commander, though she questioned whether Naiad's secrecy about the Drowned City and her father's past was protection or deception. Her actions reflected a blend of loyalty and resolve, even as she navigated political and personal conflicts.

## History
Lilianne led the Rangers out of the Deep Woods with the Old Oak's blessing, trading favors for secrets to secure resources before heading to the Drowned City. She made camp among its ruins, relying on Naiad's Rangers to replenish her supplies. She pushed into the Hinterland, establishing a presence in the Narrow Pass, where she encountered a Giant Python as an adviser. She later met Perrin at the Ruined Temple, offering him restitution for losses but was rebuffed. Her use of the Fire Talker's weapon, warned about by Naiad, culminated in ending a war, though it strained her relationships. She faced internal conflict over her actions, including banishing Sorrel to the Narrow Pass, reflecting her struggle between justice and personal bonds.
