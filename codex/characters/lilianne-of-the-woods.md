# Lilianne of the Woods

## Description

Lilianne of the Woods was a fiery and passionate leader with sharp blue eyes and black hair, wrapped in a mantel that seemed too large for her. She was driven by a strong sense of justice and a determination to fill her father's role. Her relationships were complex, often involving heated discussions with Sorrel, yet she valued Sorrel's advice in critical moments. She trusted Naiad as both a mother and commander, though she questioned whether Naiad's secrecy about the Drowned City and her father's past was protection or deception. Her actions reflected a blend of loyalty and resolve, even as she navigated political and personal conflicts.

## History

Lilianne led the Rangers out of the Deep Woods after the Old Oak had gifted her the Verdflayme, heading to the Drowned City to find answers of her father's departure. It was there that her friend Sorrel brought her news of the war in the Hinterland. Lilianne rode eastward at once, establishing a presence in the Narrow Pass, where she befriended the Giant Python, whom she named Sammy. After learning of Sorrel's plans of a coup against her, she banished Sorrel in rage.
She later met Perrin at the Ruined Temple, offering him an alliance, but was rebuffed. Her use of the Fire Talker's weapon, warned about by Naiad, culminated in ending the war at the battle that would become known as the Firestorm at Buried Giant. She faced internal conflict over her actions, including banishing Sorrel to the Narrow Pass, which finally led her to the decision to bury the Verdflayme, which over time became known as the Darkest Flame.
After she signed the Truce of Ashvale, she lived out her life peacefully in the Deep Woods. She ceeded the throne to her daughter Lito in the 364th Year of the Old Oak.
