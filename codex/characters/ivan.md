# Ivan

## Description

Ivan was the consort of Llith, the Chancellor of the Woodland Empire. His long, wavy blond hair and skill with the lute made him a popular site in the Deep Woods. A calm counterpart to Lilith's temparement, he knows more than any when to the Chancellor desires counsel and when to be silent. A pacifist with a love for relics of the past.

## History

Formerly an acolyte of the Healer's Temple in the Fertile Valley, Ivan undertook many pilgrimages to find relics of old. It is rumoured he and Lilith met in the Reliquary. The two married in the 398th Year of the Old Oak.

## Location

Deep Woods

## Status

Adviser to Lilianne
