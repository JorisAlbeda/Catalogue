# The Sorcerer of the Woods

## Description

The Sorcerer of the Woods was the Chancellor of the Woodland Empire from Oak 250 to Oak 309, when he left the throne to his daughter Lilianne. He described himself as a trouble-maker, but a treehugging trouble-maker.

## History

The Sorcerer overthrew Halvard Cray with the help of Wren Sable and swore the Oath of Protection. Eventually, he married Naiad an had one daughter, Lilianne. His powers prevented him from aging, looking ever as if he were 30, the age at which he gained his powers in the Charming Valley. In the 309th Year of the Oak, on Lilianne's 18th birthday, he abdicated, leaving her as the youngest Chancellor the Old Oak has ever known. He then left the lands, never to be seen again. Some, like Sorrel, believed him to be dead, but his wife and daughter kept hope that one day he would return.
