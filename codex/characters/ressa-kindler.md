# Ressa Kindler

## Description

Ressa Kindler was a member of the Fire Talkers, a clan known for their mastery of fire and secrets. She was known for her meticulous habit of refolding cloth scraps into exact creases, as if awaiting someone's return. Her abilities allowed her to influence a man's luck in combat, but only while holding the Darkest Secret and at the cost of a secret spent. She was associated with Fertile Valley, where she sat at Corvin Varn's table since the beginning. Her actions carried weight in trade and conflict, as seen in her dealings at Magician's Code. She bore the burden of her clan's past, including their role in Isolde Varn's downfall.

## History

Ressa Kindler's clan was linked to the Fire Talkers, who defeated Isolde Varn's army at the battle of Drowned City. She kept her reasons hidden for years before revealing her face during a trade at Magician's Code. Corvin Varn, who took the Varn name and its unpaid debt, kept her close despite her clan's history with Isolde Varn. She helped him win Fertile Valley and Boiling Lake from bandits, though he later lost control of both to Perrin Ashe. She revealed the truth about Isolde Varn's death, including the Sorcerer of the Woods's role, without prompting an apology. Her actions and secrets shaped alliances and conflicts, including her role in the payment of debts and the reclaiming of the Varn name.
