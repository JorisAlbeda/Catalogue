# Naiad

## Description

Naiad was a ranger commander and a devoted mother, balancing her roles with fierce loyalty. She led the Empire's strongest force, the Rangers, with her mother's guidance. Her quirk involved a tightrope between maternal concern and military discipline. She carried a glass orb containing Verdflayme, a weapon from the Fire Talkers, granted by the Old Oak. Naiad was a steadfast warrior and the Chancellor's most trusted counselor.

## History

Naiad served as her daughter's primary adviser in the Deep Woods since her installment as Chancellor. She hid her father's role in Isolde Varn's death, revealing it only after the war ended. Her father had killed Isolde himself at the Drowned City and spent years hunting remnants of her clan out of guilt. He vanished after receiving a letter claiming someone survived the battle, leaving Naiad uncertain of his fate. She comforted Lilianne through her grief for the men Sticky Fire killed at Buried Giant, and it was after their talk that Lilianne decided to bury the Darkest Flame.
