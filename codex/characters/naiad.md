# Naiad

## Description
Naiad was a ranger commander and a devoted mother, balancing her roles with fierce loyalty. She led the Empire's strongest force, the Rangers, with her mother's guidance. Her quirk involved a tightrope between maternal concern and military discipline. She carried a glass orb containing Verdflayme, a weapon from the Fire Talkers, granted by the Old Oak. Naiad's presence was marked by her ability to ignore skulls and boost attacks against strong defenses. Her decisions often carried weight, as seen in her refusal to repeat her father's silent wars.

## History
Naiad was taken as Lilianne's adviser in the Deep Woods, where she first appeared. She hid her father's role in Isolde Varn's death, revealing it only after the war ended. Her father had killed Isolde himself at the Drowned City and spent years hunting remnants of her clan out of guilt. He vanished after receiving a letter claiming someone survived the battle, leaving Naiad uncertain of his fate. She grieved openly for the men Sticky Fire killed at Buried Giant, choosing to bury the Flame instead of continuing his silent war. Naiad's actions reflected her rejection of her father's legacy, opting for peace over vengeance.
