# The Narrator

## Description
The Narrator was a figure who moved through various locations, keeping records and engaging in conversations that shaped the narrative. They were associated with a ledger, often interacting with key characters to relay information. Their presence was marked by a focus on uncovering truths and maintaining connections between individuals. The Narrator's actions were deliberate, often guiding others toward revelations. They were described as someone who could influence events through their words and choices. Their role was to bridge gaps between characters, ensuring the story unfolded with clarity.

## History
The Narrator's journey began with interactions at Drowned City, where they encountered Sorrel Wick and helped her navigate political and personal challenges. They were involved in conversations that clarified past events, such as the origins of Sorrel's allegiance to Lilianne of the Woods. The Narrator's work extended to other locations, including the Cradled Woods and Valleys, where they facilitated communication between leaders. They were present during critical moments, such as the exchange of messages between Sorrel and Lilianne, ensuring that intentions were conveyed accurately. Their history is intertwined with the legacy of the Bandit Queen's reign, as they documented and influenced the unfolding of events. The Narrator's role was both observational and active, shaping the narrative through their interactions.
