# Perrin Ashe

## Description

Perrin Ashe was the first Warden of Ashvale. A man of quiet resolve, his sun-cracked hands bore the marks of countless battles, and a thin scar over his right eyebrow hinted at a past injury he never spoke of. He carried himself with a stubbornness that refused to accept closed doors, treating them as puzzles to be solved rather than refusals. Though slow to trust, he was quicker to forgive than most gave him credit for. His bonds were rooted in Fertile Valley, where he returned to Bettina Marsh’s mill to seek the truth, checking if Halvard Cray’s iron key still hung on its nail. He built a life of autonomy, answering to no one but himself.

## History

Perrin Ashe was an exile who rebuilt a company from the ruins of war, never allowing it to break under his command. After some skirmishes with Corvin Varn, the two decided to instead face the Woodland Empire together. Despite offers of alliance from Chancellor Lilianne, he declined the Horned Mask, choosing independence over imperial ties. When the brutal fighting against the Empire came to a standstill, leading to a truce, he founded Ashvale, a self-governed land that answered to no authority. His military campaigns saw him lead companies into the Wastes, defeating bandits who revered the Bandit Queen.
