# Perrin Ashe

## Description
Perrin Ashe was a man of quiet resolve, his weathered travel-cloak faded to the color of dust from years of travel. His sun-cracked hands bore the marks of countless battles, and a thin scar over his right eyebrow hinted at a past injury he never spoke of. He carried himself with a stubbornness that refused to accept closed doors, treating them as puzzles to be solved rather than refusals. Though slow to trust, he was quicker to forgive than most gave him credit for. His bonds were rooted in Fertile Valley, where he returned to Bettina Marsh’s mill to seek the truth, checking if Halvard Cray’s iron key still hung on its nail. He built a life of autonomy, answering to no one but himself.

## History
Perrin Ashe was an exile who rebuilt a company from the ruins of war, never allowing it to break under his command. He refused offers of the Horned Mask, a relic from the Imperial Reliquary, choosing instead to forge his own path. After leaving the Hinterland with three provinces and a miller’s daughter’s key, he founded Ashvale, a self-governed land that answered to no authority. His military campaigns saw him lead companies into the Wastes, defeating bandits without ever setting foot there. He secured four strongholds, surpassing the Empire’s control, and warned Lilianne of the Woods to protect her interests. Despite offers of alliance, he declined the Horned Mask, choosing independence over imperial ties.
