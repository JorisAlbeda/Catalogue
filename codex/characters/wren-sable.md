# Wren Sable

## Description
Wren Sable was a figure entangled in a web of unspoken debts and unresolved grievances. She was the one who took blame for a crime that belonged to Isolde, carrying the weight of a secret until her death. Her legacy lived on through objects she left behind, like a cloth and a sealed ledger box, which others inherited and pondered. She was known for her quiet endurance, never explaining the cost of her choices to those around her. Her story was one of unacknowledged sacrifice, with no clear resolution to the debts she owed or was owed. Her name lingered in conversations, a reminder of a past that refused to stay buried.

## History
Wren Sable was caught in a conflict where she absorbed a crime meant for Isolde, who only confessed the truth on her deathbed. She bore the burden of a debt that others, like Corvin, struggled to reconcile, with no clear answers about what she had truly done. Her death marked the end of a secret that had shaped relationships and unresolved tensions. She left behind belongings that others carried forward, such as a cloth tied to her story and a ledger box marked SANCTUARY. Her actions influenced those who came after her, including Sorrel, who inherited her legacy and questions about justice. The weight of her choices remained, shaping the lives of those who tried to understand them long after she was gone.
