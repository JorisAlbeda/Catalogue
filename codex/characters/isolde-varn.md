# Isolde Varn

## Description
Isolde Varn took the Varn name as a young man, embracing both halves of an unpaid debt. He became a bandit queen, winning control of Fertile Valley and Boiling Lake during the war's opening days but losing them within a week to Perrin Ashe. His first companion on the road was Hesper, a keeper of an old way station who considered the road home more than any roof. Isolde maintained a table for Ressa Kindler despite her clan's history of conflict, learning from her the true details of his death. He led campaigns against the Sorcerer of the Woods at Drowned City, facing three charges that ultimately ended in his demise. His legacy lives in the marker at Drowned City, which names no one and apologizes to no one.

## History
Isolde Varn seized Fertile Valley and Boiling Lake from bandits early in the war but lost both territories to Perrin Ashe within a week. He kept Ressa Kindler at his table, despite her clan's history of enmity, and from her learned the full account of his death: three charges at Drowned City against the Sorcerer of the Woods. The Fire Talkers repelled the first two attacks, but the Sorcerer himself ended the third, killing Isolde. His death was shrouded in mystery until Corvin Varn, his relative, uncovered the truth through conversations with Ressa and others. The legacy of Isolde Varn is tied to Ashvale, a country formed from the Hinterland, where he is remembered only indirectly. Corvin Varn later mended what Isolde broke, leaving a marker at Drowned City that acknowledges his final words.
