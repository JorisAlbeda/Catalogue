# Sammy

## Description
Sammy was a Giant Python, appearing as a feral snake from hell yet docile to Lilianne and her rangers. He enjoyed being scratched on the head, which calmed his otherwise fierce demeanor. His status as a faceup card indicated his availability for use in battles. He was recruited during Round 3, joining Lilianne's forces at Narrow Path. His presence provided both intimidation and a sense of loyalty to those he trusted. His abilities were tied to battle strategies, offering tactical advantages in combat.

## History
Sammy was recruited at Narrow Path during Round 3, becoming part of Lilianne's rangers. His docile nature toward her and her team made him a valuable companion despite his fearsome appearance. As a faceup card, he was readily available for deployment in critical moments. His role in battles was defined by his ability to influence combat outcomes through strategic advantages. The rangers relied on his presence for both protection and morale. His integration into Lilianne's forces marked a significant shift in the dynamics of their campaigns.
