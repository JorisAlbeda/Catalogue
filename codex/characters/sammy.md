# Sammy

## Description

Sammy was a Giant Python, appearing as a feral snake from hell yet docile to Lilianne and her rangers. He enjoyed being scratched on the head, which calmed his otherwise fierce demeanor. He was recruited in the third month of the war, joining Lilianne's forces at Narrow Path. His presence provided both intimidation and a sense of loyalty to those he trusted. His abilities were tied to battle strategies, offering tactical advantages in combat.

## History

Sammy was recruited at Narrow Path, becoming part of Lilianne's rangers. His docile nature toward her and her team made him a valuable companion despite his fearsome appearance. His role in battles was defined more by intimidation than actual fighting. The rangers relied on his presence for both protection and morale. He also allowed some of them to travel on his body, carrying them great distances, as when he carried Lilianne and Naiad all the way from the Cradle to the Hinterland (now Ashvale) where they met with Perrin.
