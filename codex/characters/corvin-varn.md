# Corvin Varn

## Description

Corvin Varn was an exile with blue-grey skin, broad shoulders, and close-cropped hair that had turned prematurely grey at the temples. He wore a signet ring too large for his hand, which had been resized twice by another jeweler. His demeanor was deliberate and stubborn, treating every unanswered question as unfinished work. He avoided grief but refused to let debts remain unpaid, even when inconvenient. His presence carried a quiet intensity, often marked by a measured tone and a refusal to engage in unnecessary conflict.

## History

From the moment he reached adulthood, Corvin Varn was burdened by his inheritance: the forgotten crime committed by his grandmother, Isolde Varn, who died fighting the Sorcerer of the Woods. After his struggle with Perrin Ashe, he raised an army of his own and marched on the Woodland Empire, but was defeated by Lilianne.
At the end of the war, he became close with Perrin, and the two were often seen together as they built the new country of Ashvale.

A year after the war, he left Ashvale and traveled to the Drowned City, seeking to resolve lingering questions about his grandmother’s legacy, including her role in the exile of Wren Sable. His interactions with Sorrel Wick and Perrin Ashe revealed his determination to confront unresolved matters, even as he grappled with the weight of his name. Despite his resolve, he remained cautious, avoiding direct confrontation while seeking clarity on his past and future obligations.
