# Corvin Varn

## Description
Corvin Varn was a Blue Exile with blue-grey skin, broad shoulders, and close-cropped hair that had turned prematurely grey at the temples. He wore a signet ring too large for his hand, which had been resized twice by another jeweler. His demeanor was deliberate and stubborn, treating every unanswered question as unfinished work. He avoided grief but refused to let debts remain unpaid, even when inconvenient. His presence carried a quiet intensity, often marked by a measured tone and a refusal to engage in unnecessary conflict.

## History
Corvin Varn inherited a sealed ledger box marked SANCTUARY and the Truthwatcher's desk at Charming Valley from Wren Sable, along with an unresolved question about whether Isolde Varn ever deserved her apology. After the war, he left Ashvale and traveled to Fertile Valley, where he settled near the Ruined Temple. He sought to resolve lingering questions about his grandmother’s legacy, including her role in events tied to the Temple. His interactions with Sorrel Wick and Perrin Ashe revealed his determination to confront unresolved matters, even as he grappled with the weight of his name. Despite his resolve, he remained cautious, avoiding direct confrontation while seeking clarity on his past and future obligations.
