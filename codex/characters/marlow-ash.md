# Marlow Ash

## Description
Marlow Ash was a quiet reader of leftovers, known for peering into discard piles to uncover truths others discarded. He treated the discarded items of others as more honest than what they kept, a quirk that shaped his approach to secrets and power. His actions often revolved around reclaiming what was lost or overlooked, whether through negotiation, strategy, or direct confrontation. He was associated with the Charming Valley and Drowned City, where his decisions carried weight. His reputation as a Scryer allowed him to access discarded knowledge, which he used to navigate political and personal conflicts. Marlow Ash was a figure of ambiguity, balancing debts and alliances with a patience that masked his intentions.

## History
Marlow Ash was drawn to Charming Valley through a Search action, where he played to advisers and began his role as a Scryer. He left nine companies of his own people to hold Charming Valley without him, walking south alone to Drowned City to claim what the ruin owed. His actions there were marked by a refusal to settle for half-measures, leading to confrontations that solidified his influence. He owed Daz of Narrow Pass a debt, sending two of his own people as a gesture without expectation of payment. His interactions with Lilianne were fraught, as she questioned his loyalty and intentions, yet he remained steadfast in his pursuits. Marlow Ash’s history is one of quiet persistence, using discarded secrets to shape outcomes while avoiding the full weight of power.
