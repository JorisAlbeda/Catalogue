# Fenn

## Description
Fenn was a skilled negotiator who could persuade caravans to take indirect routes through friendly territory. He counted wagon wheels under his breath before each departure, claiming it was for luck. He remained close to Perrin at Fertile Valley, acting as a quiet advisor rather than a direct combatant. Fenn was unafraid to send messages to enemies, even when it risked personal danger. He was known for his ability to navigate complex political situations with tact. His actions often influenced the outcomes of critical decisions without direct confrontation.

## History
Fenn first appeared at Fertile Valley's side early in the conflict, already aligned with Perrin. He helped secure Daz's men by offering coin, ensuring their loyalty without direct conflict. Fenn mediated discussions about strategic movements, prioritizing caution over aggression. He delivered critical messages to the Chancellor unescorted, risking his safety to convey Perrin's demands. Fenn facilitated alliances by sending updates to Sorrel Wick, ensuring she remained informed without interference. His final act involved crossing the Hinterland alone, trading secrets at a forgotten way station to secure temporary ground.
