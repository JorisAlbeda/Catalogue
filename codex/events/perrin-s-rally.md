# Perrin's rally

## Description

Perrin's rally was a gathering of companies assembled by Perrin Ashe at the river fork in the Fertile Valley. Companies had followed him for two seasons without knowing their purpose, but he called them together to reaffirm their commitment. Bettina Marsh observed from the mill, her presence a reminder of her longstanding connection to him. This act solidified their unity and purpose, reinforcing their bond under his leadership.

## History

The rally originated as Perrin Ashe's effort to consolidate his forces after years of wandering. He had previously rebuilt his company in Charming Valley, where Corvin faced challenges reclaiming lost assets. The event occurred in the third month of the war, following a period of strategic reinforcement at both Fertile Valley and the Mine. Perrin's declaration of the charter's name was a symbolic act that tied their cause to Halvard Cray's legacy. Earlier conflicts, such as Lilianne's attack on Charming Valley, highlighted the necessity of such gatherings. The rally also reflected Perrin's growing influence, as Bettina Marsh remained at the mill, watching his actions unfold. This event became a cornerstone in their shared history, shaping their future actions and alliances.
