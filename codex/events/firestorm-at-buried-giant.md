# The Firestorm at Buried Giant

## Description

The Firestorm at Buried Giant was a decisive battle at the end of the war against the Bandit Queen. Sorrel's forces were camped at Buried Giant. Nine companies defended the site, and all were brutally slaughtered by the Chancellor's Verdflayme. The battle's aftermath saw the consolidation of relics and banners in the Chancellor's strongbox, symbolizing the war's end. This event is remembered as a turning point where the conflict's momentum shifted irreversibly.

## History

The battle emerged from Sorrel's decision to stay at Buried Giant, refusing to abandon the site after her exile from the Woodland Empire. In the last month of the war, Lilianne of the Woods attacked the site with a large force, bolstered with the Darkest Flame. The horror of its slaughter led to Lilianne's decision to bury the Darkest Flame and create a memorial, in honour of the lives lost.
