# Meeting at Ruined Temple

## Description
Meeting at Ruined Temple was a gathering where Perrin Ashe, Sorrel Wick, and Corvin Varn convened to discuss terms following the war. The temple, a crumbling remnant of Perrin's grandfather's former empire, served as a neutral ground untouched by the conflict. Sorrel chose the location herself, finding it unclaimed and symbolic of the old order's decline. The meeting occurred under the shadow of the temple's broken structure, where past power once stood. Perrin arrived early, reflecting on his family's lost legacy, while Sorrel observed the site's remnants with quiet intent. The conversation unfolded between them, marked by mutual wariness and the weight of unspoken history.

## History
The Ruined Temple became a meeting place two months after the truce, as Perrin's new realm of Ashvale expanded. Sorrel, seeking answers, selected the temple as a neutral ground, untouched by the new order. Perrin, reflecting on his grandfather's fallen empire, invited her there to discuss her intentions. Corvin joined the meeting, his presence tied to his grandmother's legacy and the unresolved past. The temple's stones bore witness to their deliberations, a place where old power yielded to new alliances. After the meeting, Sorrel left with an offer of land, while Corvin later returned to the Drowned City, marking the temple's role in shaping the post-war era.
