# The banishment

## Description
The banishment was a formal exile imposed on Sorrel Wick by Lilianne, declaring her an outcast from the Empire. It was a decisive act to settle a debt, executed without hesitation or negotiation. Sorrel was given three days to leave Buried Giant, marking the end of her political standing and influence.

## History
Lilianne ordered Sorrel's banishment after she refused to defend Buried Giant, choosing to burn her own followers instead of engaging in battle. This act of defiance confirmed Lilianne's belief that Sorrel was no longer a viable ally. The banishment was finalized in Round 8, following Sorrel's second exile, leaving her with no banner, crown, or remaining power.
