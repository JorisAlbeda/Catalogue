# War's end

## Description
War's end did not arrive as foretold, with Sorrel's forces remaining at Buried Giant instead of retreating. Four companies stayed to defend the position while three returned to Deep Woods. The conflict persisted beyond its initial conclusion, delayed by days until the final reckoning. War Exhaustion sealed the war's fate, ending all further action before decisions could be made. Relics and banners, including the People's Favor and Darkest Secret, were stored in the Chancellor's strongbox, symbolizing the war's resolution. The Empire retained control of key territories while ceding others to new names, marking the transition to peace.

## History
The war concluded not as expected, with Sorrel's warbands refusing to abandon Buried Giant. Four companies remained to hold the ground, while three followed her to Deep Woods. The final battle unfolded later than anticipated, with Sorrel's forces ultimately prevailing. War Exhaustion ended the conflict abruptly, preventing further escalation. The Chancellor's strongbox contained relics and banners, including the People's Favor and Darkest Secret, signifying the war's conclusion. In the aftermath, the Empire transferred Fertile Valley, the Mine, Narrow Pass, and Wastes to Ashvale under truce. Sorrel sought reconciliation at the Ruined Temple, where the new nation's unclaimed territories awaited resolution.
