# The burial of the Darkest Flame

## Description

The burial of the Darkest Flame was a ritual to end a cycle of violence by symbolically entombing the weapon Sticky Fire. Lilianne vowed to build a memorial at the site, transforming it into a symbol of peace rather than a reminder of war. This act marked a shift from vengeance to devotion to the people's well-being.

## History

Lilianne decided to bury the Flame after a conversation with her mother Naiad, who contrasted her choice with her father's silent carrying of the weapon. Corvin Varn acknowledged her decision, noting it as a significant act of burying the fire and swearing a new oath. The event became tied to the legacy of ending conflict and redefining loyalty.
