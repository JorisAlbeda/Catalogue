# The banner being taken back

## Description
The banner was taken back through a series of transactions and conflicts involving Sorrel, Corvin, and Lilianne. Sorrel initially lost the banner to Corvin but later bought it back, only for it to be taken again by Lilianne. The banner's final possession shifted multiple times before settling in the Chancellor's hands.

## History
Sorrel lost the banner to Corvin, but she bought it back using accumulated favor, only for Corvin to reclaim it later. Lilianne then took the banner from Corvin, and it eventually ended up in the Chancellor's possession after a series of disputes. The banner's movement became a focal point of power struggles among the factions.

Tinker's Fair
