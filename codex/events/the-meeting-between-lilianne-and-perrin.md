# The meeting between Lilianne and Perrin

## Description
Lilianne and Perrin met at the Ruined Temple, where she offered him restored lands, a seat at her table, and a relic from his grandfather's past. Perrin rejected all her gifts and openly named her his enemy before departing. The encounter marked a turning point in their conflict, solidifying their opposing roles.

## History
Lilianne traveled three regions to meet Perrin at the Ruined Temple, presenting him with the valley's restoration and a symbolic mask. Perrin refused her offerings, choosing to stand against her. She declared him her enemy, ending their brief truce and escalating their rivalry. This meeting preceded Perrin's subsequent attacks on Narrow Pass.
