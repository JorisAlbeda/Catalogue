# The letter from Halvard

## Description
The letter from Halvard was a single folded missive discovered in a strongbox hidden beneath a millstone. It was written in Halvard's own hand, addressing an unknown recipient with a warning about mistaking the valley for the thing worth keeping. The letter's contents hinted at a deeper truth about the region's history and its rulers.

## History
Perrin Ashe uncovered the letter during a confrontation with Corvin Varn over Fertile Valley, after retrieving a key from a nail. He found it in a strongbox that had remained sealed for decades, with no official documents but Halvard's personal message. The letter was later returned to its hiding place, as Perrin believed its message was meant for future seekers.

## Location
Fertile Valley
