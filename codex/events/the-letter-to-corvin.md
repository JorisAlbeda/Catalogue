# The letter to Corvin

## Description
The letter to Corvin was a message left with Bettina, instructing him to seek out what Sorrel herself admitted she did not have the right to find on Wren's behalf. It guided Corvin's journey south from Ashvale after the Ruined Temple, tying his actions to unresolved tensions involving the Chancellor and a key. The letter's contents remained central to his quest despite later exchanges with Perrin.

## History
Sorrel sent the letter to Corvin before the war, which he followed to Ashvale, though its full purpose remained unclear. Corvin later received a separate letter from Perrin, revealing the Chancellor had taken his prize back, but the original letter remained tied to his pursuit of the key and unresolved debts. The letter's existence underscored the friction between Corvin, Perrin, and the Chancellor's ambitions.
