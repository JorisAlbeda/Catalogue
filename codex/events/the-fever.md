# The fever

## Description
The fever was a dangerous condition associated with Boiling Lake, where an ever-boiling pot demanded respect from those who dared to approach. Those who entered without permission faced dire consequences, often losing their lives or being left as warnings to others. The lake itself became a symbol of both peril and necessity for those who relied on its resources.

## History
The fever originated from Brack's relentless tending of the pot at Boiling Lake, a practice that ensured the lake's power remained untamed. Intruders who ignored the warnings met swift violence, their fates serving as grim reminders of the lake's wrath. Over time, the fever became a legend, blending fact with fear in the stories of those who lived near its reach.
