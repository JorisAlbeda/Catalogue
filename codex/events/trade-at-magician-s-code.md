# Trade at Magician's Code

## Description
Trade at Magician's Code involved exchanging secrets for favors and strategic advantages. Participants risked their secrets to gain access to resources or influence, often at great personal cost. The event became a pivotal moment for those involved, shaping alliances and power dynamics. Secrets were traded openly, with some willing to burn their last secret for a chance at reward. The exchange carried weight, as it could determine the outcome of conflicts or negotiations. It was a calculated risk, balancing potential gains against the loss of personal information.

## History
The event originated from Ressa Kindler's decision to reveal her true face before a high-stakes trade at Magician's Code. She had long kept her intentions hidden, but the opportunity to secure a significant advantage compelled her to act. The trade allowed her to gain leverage, enabling her to influence events across regions like Charming Valley and the Cradle. It was part of a broader strategy to rebuild strength and secure favors, as seen in her later actions at Tinker's Fair and the Forest Council. The event's impact extended beyond individual gains, affecting larger political and military maneuvers. Its legacy is tied to the balance of power and the willingness to sacrifice secrets for control.
