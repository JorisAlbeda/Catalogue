# Halvard Cray's war

## Description
Halvard Cray's war was his campaign to reclaim his grandfather's conquests and rule more land than anyone else. He declared his ambition openly after reinforcing Fertile Valley and the Mine, asserting his claim to greater territory. The war began when he gathered companies to fight under his name, naming it 'Halvard Cray's war' at Fertile Valley.

## History
Halvard Cray's war started when he took the Mine as his second stronghold and reinforced Fertile Valley. He publicly named the war after himself at Fertile Valley, declaring his intent to surpass the Chancellor's holdings. Later, he rejected the Chancellor's offers of citizenship and openly declared himself an enemy, escalating tensions as the conflict expanded.
