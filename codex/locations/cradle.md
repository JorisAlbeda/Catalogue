# Cradle

## Description

The Cradle was a region where Lilianne of the Woods held her father's throne, marked by the legacy of governance and the weight of inherited responsibility. It was a place where the Old Oak's influence lingered, as evidenced by the Verdflayme orb granted to Lilianne, symbolizing a connection to the forest's ancient power. The Cradle remained under the Empire's control, though it was not among the territories surrendered to Ashvale. Its rulers faced the challenge of maintaining order while navigating the complexities of legacy and new oaths. The region was associated with the Deep Woods, where Lilianne's rule was tested by both internal and external forces.

## History

The Cradle was part of the Empire's holdings before the truce that saw Fertile Valley, the Mine, Narrow Pass, and Wastes ceded to Ashvale. Lilianne of the Woods became its Oathkeeper at eighteen, inheriting her father's throne and the burden of leadership. Her rule was shaped by the memory of her father's reign and the expectations of the Old Oak's legacy. The Cradle's history intertwined with the broader conflicts of the Empire, as it was a site of both governance and the unresolved tensions between factions. Sorrel Wick's return to the Ruined Temple and the eventual settlement of claims in the new country reflected the Cradle's role in the shifting balance of power. The region's fate was sealed by the Empire's decision to retain what it held, leaving the Cradle under Lilianne's stewardship.
