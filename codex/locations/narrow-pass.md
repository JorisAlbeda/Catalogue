# Narrow Pass

## Description
Narrow Pass was a defile in the Hinterland, a narrow road that served as the only passage through the region from one side to the other. It was a relic of ancient construction, its stone walls holding the memory of battles long past. The pass became a strategic stronghold, where bandits and warbands established their presence, making it a contested ground. A Giant Python, a formidable creature, made its lair within the pass, guarding its secrets. The area was known for its deadly confrontations, where warriors clashed in fierce battles. Its name became synonymous with conflict, as it shaped the fates of those who dared to traverse its path.

## History
Narrow Pass was established as a critical route through the Hinterland, where Lilianne made her camp with three warbands, solidifying her control over the region. It became a battleground where Perrin fought multiple encounters, including a decisive clash that reshaped his strategies. The pass was fortified by bandits who resisted being overpowered, leading to skirmishes that tested the resolve of those who challenged them. A legend emerged about a woman named Wick, whose actions may have influenced the bandits to become more disciplined. The pass's strategic value made it a focal point of conflict, with its stone walls bearing witness to the bloodshed of warriors. Its role in the war solidified its place in history as a site of both peril and power.
