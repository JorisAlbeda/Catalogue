# Narrow Pass

## Description

Narrow Pass was a defile in Ashvale, a narrow road that served as the only passage from the Woodland Empire to Ashvale. The pass became a strategic stronghold, where bandits and warbands established their presence, making it a contested ground. A Giant Python, later tamed by Lilianne, made its lair within the pass, guarding its secrets. The pass was long ruled by bandits under Daz the Bandit Witch.

## History

Before Ashvale was founded, Narrow Pass was a critical route through the Hinterland. Lilianne travelled here on a quest to find the Bandit Crown. It was here that she was dubbed The Bandit Queen by Daz, gaining her the allegiance of all bandit clans.
Perrin eventually chased the bandits out of the pass, and after the truce, it became the official border between Ashvale and the Woodland Empire.
It is speculated that the Narrow Pass is the most important reason that Ashvale was never invaded during the war with Lilianne, or in the century of relative peace that followed.
