# The forest

## Description
The forest was a vast expanse where the Forest Council held sway, its influence stretching across regions. It was a place of ancient trees and hidden paths, where travelers relied on favors to navigate its depths. The Old Oak, a revered tree within the forest, was said to grant secrets in exchange for loyalty. The forest's inhabitants, including Rangers and Oathkeepers, maintained its balance through tradition and duty. It was a realm of both danger and resource, where survival depended on knowledge of its hidden ways. The forest's presence shaped the fates of those who sought its aid or crossed its borders.

## History
The forest was once a sanctuary for those who followed the Old Oak's guidance, its paths used to move between distant regions like Deep Woods and Charming Valley. It played a key role in the war, as Lilianne traversed its paths to secure allies and resources, trading favors for passage. The Forest Council, an enigmatic body, ensured no enemy of Charming Valley found refuge within its borders. Over time, the forest became a battleground, with its paths serving as routes for armies and spies. Its legacy endures in the traditions of those who once walked its woods, from the Rangers to the Oathkeepers. Even after the war, the forest remained a place of whispered secrets and unbroken oaths.
