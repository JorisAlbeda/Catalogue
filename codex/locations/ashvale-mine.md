# Ashvale Mine

## Description

The Ashvale Mine was a place of quiet labor and unspoken tensions, where the weight of past decisions lingered in the air. Once part of the Woodland Empire, it later was a source of great wealth to Ashvale after the Truce.

## History

Ashvale Mine was once a contested territory, claimed by those who sought to control its resources and the power it represented. In the war against the Woodland Empire, Perrin and Corvin fought each other over control over the Mine's riches, with Perrin eventually securing the site and setting up a mine camp there that would later grow into an active mining town once peace had settled over Ashvale.
