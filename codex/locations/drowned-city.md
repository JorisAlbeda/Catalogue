# Drowned City

## Description

Drowned City lay a month's ride south of Ashvale, back across the Narrow Pass into the old Empire's territory. Its towers had long since succumbed to the sea, their remnants bleached white by salt and time. Gulls circled the water's edge, where the city's foundations had crumbled into the waves. A heron stood sentinel where a wall once stood, its presence a quiet testament to the place's forgotten grandeur. The air carried a salt breeze instead of the smoke that once filled the air. Corvin Varn visited it after the truce, finding it quieter than any painting had ever shown, its secrets still lingering in the drowned ruins.

## History

Drowned City had once been a stronghold of the Empire, yet no document remains describing what happened to it or in what era it flourished. The location is known as the site where the battle of the Sorcerer of the Woods was decided, as he and his Fire Talkers cast down Isolde Varn's armies, killing her in the battle.

Lilianne of the Woods returned here several times to find out secrets of her missing father. Corvin Varn crossed its threshold for the first time after the Truce of Ashvale, standing where his grandmother had died. Even now, the city's silence hinted at truths yet to be unearthed, its drowned foundations holding echoes of a bygone era.
