# The Great Spire

## Description

The Great Spire was a tower on Buried Giant. Built around a small memorial to symbolize peace, it served to protect the Darkest Flame.
The memorial was a structure built by Lilianne to symbolize peace after a battle, constructed with the intent to bury the Flame and end the cycle of violence. It stood as a reminder of the conflict's conclusion rather than its tragedy, with the Flame interred beside the Giant. Its design reflected a commitment to the people's well-being, marking a shift from warfare to reconciliation.

## History

The memorial was created following the Firestorm at Buried Giant, where Lilianne chose to bury the Flame and establish a symbol of peace. She vowed to swear a new Oath, dedicating herself to the people's welfare. The site became a focal point for reflections on the past, with figures like Sorrel and Corvin visiting to confront their personal connections to the events. In the 341st year of the Old Oak, following an attempted theft of the Darkest Flame, a tower was constructed around the memorial, which came to be known as the Great Spire.
