# Cradled Woods and Valleys

## Description
Cradled Woods and Valleys was a region where the Old Oak and Forest Paths connected key locations, serving as a strategic nexus for movement and influence. Its valleys and woods were traversed by those seeking favor or power, with the Cradle itself acting as a bridge between distant territories. The area held significance as a place where political and military decisions shaped the fate of nearby regions.

## History
The Cradle became central to political maneuvering as Lilianne seated a Special Envoy at Charming Valley's table from its vantage point. Corvin later reclaimed Charming Valley, undoing years of work in a single afternoon, highlighting the region's role in contested power. It remained a focal point for negotiations, with figures like Sorrel and Perrin referencing its history in their struggles for control.
