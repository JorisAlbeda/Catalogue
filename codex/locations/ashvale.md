# Ashvale

## Description
A country in the Woodland Empire's maps where the Hinterland used to be. It is a self-governed entity with its own borders, roads, harvests, and a ruling line that keeps the story of the mask. The region is marked by the Ruined Temple, the Buried Giant, and the stolen flame, while its history is shaped by the truce between the Empire and its former territories.

## History
Formed after the war, Ashvale was established through a truce where the Empire relinquished control of Fertile Valley, Mine, Narrow Pass, and Wastes to a new name. Perrin Ashe, with the support of Corvin Varn, unified these lands under Ashvale's banner. The Ruined Temple, the last remains of the Empire's former rule, became a symbolic meeting ground for Perrin and Corvin. Ashvale's histories focus on the legacy of Corvin Varn, the Fire Talkers' debates over the stolen flame, and the unclaimed Mask in the Reliquary, which questions the meaning of symbols versus their deeds.
