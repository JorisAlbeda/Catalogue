# The memorial

## Description

The memorial was a structure built to symbolize peace after a battle, constructed with the intent to bury the Flame and end the cycle of violence. It stood as a reminder of the conflict's conclusion rather than its tragedy, with the Flame interred beside the Giant. Its design reflected a commitment to the people's well-being, marking a shift from warfare to reconciliation.

## History

The memorial was created following a decisive battle, where Lilianne chose to bury the Flame and establish a symbol of peace. She vowed to swear a new Oath, dedicating herself to the people's welfare. The site became a focal point for reflections on the past, with figures like Sorrel and Corvin visiting to confront their personal connections to the events.
