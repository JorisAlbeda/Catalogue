# Ruined Temple

## Description
The Ruined Temple stood as the last remnants of an empire once ruled by Perrin's grandfather. Its stone walls, broken and moss-covered, hinted at a grander past, with inscriptions worn by time and weather. The structure resembled a hall, likely a throne room, though its original purpose was lost to history. Sorrel traced faded carvings on a fallen lintel, acknowledging its former significance. Perrin, upon arriving, recognized the site's historical weight despite its decay. The temple's location in Fertile Valley marked it as an unclaimed space within the newly formed Ashvale.

## History
Built during the reign of Perrin's grandfather, the temple symbolized imperial authority before the empire's collapse. After the war, it became a neutral ground for negotiations, chosen by Sorrel to meet Perrin and Corvin. Perrin, now Warden of Ashvale, used the site to reaffirm his leadership, offering land and autonomy to those who sought it. The temple's ruins were left untouched, a testament to the empire's fall and the new order's respect for its legacy. Sorrel's visit marked a turning point, as she relinquished her claim to power and chose a quiet retreat. The temple's existence persisted as a silent witness to the shifting tides of power in the region.
