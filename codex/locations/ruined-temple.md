# Ruined Temple

## Description

The Ruined Temple stood as the last remnants of the Broken Empire. Its stone walls, broken and moss-covered, hinted at a grander past, with inscriptions worn by time and weather. The structure resembled a hall, likely a throne room, though its original purpose was lost to history. The temple's location in Fertile Valley marked it as a monument of great significance in Ashvale.

## History

Built during the reign of the Broken Empire, the temple symbolized imperial authority before the empire's collapse. It was often used as a neutral ground for negotiations, chosen by Lilianne to meet Perrin, and later chosen by Sorrel to meet Perrin and Corvin. Perrin, after becoming Warden of Ashvale, used the site to reaffirm his leadership, offering land and autonomy to those who sought it.

Fertile Valley
