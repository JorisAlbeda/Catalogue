# Fertile Valley

## Description
Fertile Valley was a valley where Corvin arrived to find it occupied by bandits, with Perrin Ashe already entrenched there. The valley's fertile land was contested, and its location near Boiling Lake made it a strategic site for gathering companies. It was a place of conflict and negotiation, where Corvin faced both bandits and Perrin's claim. The valley's markets were revitalized through trade, allowing Corvin to rebuild his forces. Its natural resources, including the boiling lake, were central to its significance. The valley's history was marked by shifting allegiances and the presence of figures like Brack, who tended the lake.

## History
Fertile Valley was first contested by Corvin and Perrin Ashe, who both claimed the land without prior agreement. Corvin's attempts to secure the valley led to conflicts, including battles against bandits and rival claimants. Perrin later used the valley as a rallying point, gathering companies under his banner. The valley's strategic importance drew attention from figures like Lilianne, who later took control of Charming Valley. Corvin eventually left Fertile Valley after resolving his disputes, abandoning it to Perrin's claim. The valley's fate was tied to the Mine and Hinterland, but its history remained rooted in its role as a contested ground for power and survival.
