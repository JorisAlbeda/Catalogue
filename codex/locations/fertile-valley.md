# Fertile Valley

## Description

Fertile Valley was the capital of Ashvale, known for the Boiling Lake and the Ruined Temple, a remnant of a time when it was the most important location in the Supreme Empire.

## History

Fertile Valley was first contested by Corvin and Perrin Ashe, who both claimed the land without prior agreement. Corvin's attempts to secure the valley led to conflicts, including battles against bandits and rival claimants. Perrin later used the valley as a rallying point, gathering companies under his banner. The Ruined Temple was later used as a meeting point for several important discussions about the future of the land.
