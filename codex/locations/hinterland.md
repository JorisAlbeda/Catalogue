# Hinterland

## Description
Hinterland was a region defined by its treacherous terrain and strategic significance. Its narrow roadways, such as Narrow Pass, served as critical entry points for armies and travelers. The area was marked by natural hazards, including dense forests and perilous rivers, which made movement difficult. It was also home to ancient ruins and hidden settlements, like the Ruined Temple and Drowned City. The presence of the Buried Giant and other mysterious landmarks added to its enigmatic reputation. Warbands and political factions often contested control of its resources and trade routes.

## History
Hinterland became a focal point during conflicts over power and territory. Lilianne established camps there, using its terrain to her advantage. Narrow Pass was a contested route, with forces vying for dominance. The region witnessed the rise and fall of alliances, such as Sorrel Wick's efforts to protect Charming Valley. The mine in the Deep Woods and the Ruined Temple were sites of both conflict and intrigue. Over time, the region's legacy was preserved through superstitions and artifacts, like the painting of Sorrel Wick, ensuring its place in history.
