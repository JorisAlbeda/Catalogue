# Corvin's camp

## Description
Corvin's camp was established in Fertile Valley after his last man fell, leaving him alone on the valley's ground. He made camp near the mill and Bettina's key, which remained unvisited, while keeping a second omen hidden among his trusted company. The camp became a temporary stronghold as he awaited reinforcements and sought answers about the key and the charter.

## History
Corvin's camp in Fertile Valley was created after he defeated his final adversary, though he later moved to the Hinterland to investigate an empty mine. He traded his last secret at a way station he had built but never used, establishing a new camp where he held two pieces of ground for the first time. The camp served as a base for his operations until he sent survivors to the Mine itself and repositioned his forces.

## Location
Fertile Valley
