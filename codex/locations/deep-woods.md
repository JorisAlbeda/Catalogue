# Deep Woods

## Description

Deep Woods was the capital of the Woodland Empire. It served as a base for Lilianne's Rangers and a place where the ledger of conflicts was recorded. The area was known for its dense foliage and the presence of the Old Oak, which held significance in the region's history. Warbands from Buried Giant occasionally passed through, and the forest was a site of strategic importance. The Deep Woods were also linked to the Provinces, with movements between the two regions facilitated by the Forest Paths.

## History

Six months into her reign as Chancellor, Lilianne of the Woods was gifted the Verdflayme, or Sticky Fire, by the Old Oak. It was from here that she first set out to war with her Rangers, her supplies always coming from the Cradle.
