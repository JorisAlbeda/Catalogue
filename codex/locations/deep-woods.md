# Deep Woods

## Description
Deep Woods was a forested region closely tied to the Old Oak's grove, where secrets were exchanged for favors. It served as a base for Lilianne's Rangers and a place where the ledger of conflicts was recorded. The area was known for its dense foliage and the presence of the Old Oak, which held significance in the region's history. Warbands from Buried Giant occasionally passed through, and the forest was a site of strategic importance. The Deep Woods were also linked to the Cradle, with movements between the two regions facilitated by the Forest Paths.

## History
Deep Woods emerged as a key location during the Oath Chronicle, where Lilianne of the Woods led her Rangers with the Old Oak's blessing. Favors were traded for secrets, enabling her to secure resources like Sticky Fire from mill-ruins and later access the Drowned City. The forest was a site of conflict, as Sorrel Wick's warbands clashed here, with some staying to defend Buried Giant while others returned to Deep Woods' ledger. The region's connection to the Cradle was evident through diplomatic efforts, such as seating a Special Envoy at Charming Valley's table. War Exhaustion eventually ended the era, leaving relics and banners in the Chancellor's strongbox, with Deep Woods remaining a place of strategic and symbolic significance.
