# Reliquary

## Description
The Reliquary was a place where relics of power and history were kept, its walls lined with artifacts that held significance beyond their material value. It was known for its role in preserving objects tied to the Imperial court, such as the Horned Mask, which remained untouched for generations. The Reliquary was a site of quiet reverence, where offerings and secrets were stored rather than displayed. Its location was shrouded in mystery, though it was closely associated with the Chancellor's domain. The air within it carried a weight of unspoken oaths, and its chambers were said to echo with the whispers of those who had once sought its treasures. It stood as a symbol of the Empire's lingering influence, even as its power waned.

## History
The Reliquary was established as a repository for relics deemed too dangerous or sacred to be used, a decision made by the Imperial court to preserve their legacy. The Horned Mask, once offered to an Exile named Ashe, was refused three times and left there instead of being forced upon anyone. Over time, the Reliquary became a place where secrets were buried alongside artifacts, its records incomplete and its purpose obscured by the passage of time. The Chancellor, Lilianne of the Woods, later referenced it as a source of relics, though its exact location remained unclear. Despite its abandonment by the Empire, the Reliquary endured, its legacy tied to the fate of those who had sought to claim its treasures. It remained a silent witness to the shifting power of the region, its existence a reminder of the past.
