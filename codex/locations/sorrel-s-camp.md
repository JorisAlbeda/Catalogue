# Sorrel's camp

## Description
Sorrel's camp was established in the Narrow Pass with three warbands supporting her. She returned from Drowned City bearing a secret instead of coin, using all her resources to secure the Banner of the People's Favor. The camp served as her base of operations before her eventual downfall.

## History
Sorrel set up camp in the Narrow Pass after acquiring the banner through a costly bargain, believing it to be a true prize. Her warbands at Charming Valley failed to protect the banner, which had already been purchased by another party. After being banished to the Narrow Pass, she was left with nothing but her remaining secrets and a shattered vision of faith.

## Location
Narrow Pass
