# The People's Favor

## Description

The People's Favor was a banner symbolizing authority and allegiance, wielded by those who could command loyalty and resources. It granted access to powerful networks, enabling its holder to secure alliances and influence key locations. The banner was traded and reclaimed multiple times, reflecting its value as both a tool and a prize.

## History

The banner was first seized by Sorrel, who was later cast out for holding it. Corvin later acquired it by paying a high price, but Lilianne eventually claimed it. Its ownership shifted hands several times, always tied to moments of significant political or military maneuvering.

## Location

Charming Valley
