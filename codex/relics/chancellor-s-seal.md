# Chancellor's Seal

## Description
The Chancellor's Seal was a royal insignia used to authenticate official correspondence and declarations of authority. It was a symbol of the Chancellor's power, often affixed to letters and decrees to legitimize their commands. The seal's presence signified the Chancellor's direct involvement in matters of governance and conflict.

## History
The seal was first employed by Chancellor Lilianne to validate her proclamations, including her claim to rule based on the realm's darkest secrets. It became a critical tool in her efforts to consolidate power, as seen in messages sent to allies and adversaries alike. Its use extended to sealing warnings and ultimatums, such as the demand for a meeting at the Ruined Temple.
