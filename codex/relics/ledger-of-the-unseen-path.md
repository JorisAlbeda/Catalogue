# Ledger of the Unseen Path

## Description

The Ledger of the Unseen Path was a weathered parchment bound in faded leather, its edges singed and pages brittle with age. It recorded the movements and dealings of those who traversed the Narrow Pass, revealing glimpses of unspoken agreements and silent wars. Its ink bore witness to the weight of pacts and the cost of passage through the region.

## History

The ledger was created by Sorrel for those who moved through the Narrow Pass without leaving a mark, their names etched only in the margins of history. It became a quiet testament to the silent governance that shaped the region's fate. The Archivists secured it, preserving its account of unseen forces that dictated the flow of power and movement.

## Location

Narrow Pass
