# Cast — Blue

## Hesper (Memory of Home)

- Status: faceup (Round 6, flipped ahead of a Trade at Tinker's Fair for the matching bonus)
- First appeared: Setup
- Ability: Action — move all favor from any one favor bank to the Nomad favor bank.
- Ability cost: Burn 1 secret
- Quirk: Refolds any scrap of cloth she's handed back into the same creases, every time, as though it still belongs to someone who's coming back for it.

## Ressa Kindler (Fire Talkers)

- Status: faceup (Round 5, flipped ahead of a Trade at Arcane-suited Magician's Code for the matching bonus)
- First appeared: Round 1, drawn via Search at Fertile Valley
- Ability: ±3 to a die roll, but only while holding the Darkest Secret.
- Ability cost: Play 1 secret
- Quirk: Never raises her voice, but always sits facing the door — like she's still expecting someone to come collect an old debt in blood.

## Old Marta (Vision of Rebellion)

- Status: revealed (moved from facedown adviser to Corvin's Revealed Vision space, Round 4 — no longer an adviser slot)
- First appeared: Round 3, drawn via Search at the Mine
- Ability: WAKE — wins the game outright if Corvin holds the People's Favor with at least three Visions drawn from the world deck (currently 4, already satisfied).
- Ability cost: None (a Wake condition, not an activated power)
- Quirk: Answers every question with another question, as though the future were something you had to bargain your way toward rather than be told.

## Sergeant Colm Rooke (Battle Honors)

- Status: faceup
- First appeared: Round 4, drawn via Search at the Mine
- Ability: If Corvin is victorious in a Campaign, gain 1 Favor from the Order bank.
- Ability cost: None (triggers on victory, no cost to use)
- Quirk: Keeps a list of every commendation he was never actually given, and reads from it before any fight whether asked to or not.
