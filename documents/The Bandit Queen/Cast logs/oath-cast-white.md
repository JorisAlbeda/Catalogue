# Cast — White

## Ossa

- Source card: Threatening Roar
- Quirk: Blunt to the point of rudeness — says the thing everyone else is too polite to say.
- Ability: When played, discard all Nomad and Beast cards at sites in your region.
- Ability Cost: None — a "When played" trigger, not an activated action.
- Status: facedown
- First appeared: Setup — taken as Sorrel's starting adviser at Charming Valley.

## Fen Quill

- Source card: Sleight of Hand
- Quirk: Light-fingered even in conversation — can't resist testing what people don't want taken.
- Ability: Action — take a Secret from a player whose Pawn is at your site. You cannot take their last Secret.
- Ability Cost: 1 favor
- Status: active (flipped faceup Round 2)
- First appeared: Round 1 — drawn via Search at Charming Valley, played to advisers.

## Marlow Ash

- Source card: Scryer
- Quirk: Treats what everyone else threw away as more honest than what they kept.
- Ability: Action — peek at any one discard pile.
- Ability Cost: 1 Secret
- Status: active
- First appeared: Round 2 — drawn via Search of the Cradle discard pile at Charming Valley, played to advisers.

(Dennet Fife / Pied Piper — recruited this Round 6 Search, then retracted: Ruined Temple's standing effect bars everyone but Perrin from playing Beast cards, and Pied Piper turned out to be Beast-suited despite its Order-adjacent look. Never actually joined the table.)
