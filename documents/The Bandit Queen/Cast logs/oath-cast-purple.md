# Cast — Purple

## Naiad

- Source card: Rangers
- Quirk: Walking a tight balance between the role of a concerned mother and a loyal and fierce commander of the rangers.
- Status: faceup
- First appeared: Setup — taken as Lilianne's starting adviser at Deep Woods.
- Ability: (Attacker Battle Plan) Ignore all skulls you roll; +-2 attack dice if the defense pool has 4+ defence dice.
- Ability cost: 1 favor

## Sammy

- Source card: Giant Python
- Quirk: Looks like a feral snake from hell, but very docile to Lilianne and her rangers. Likes head scratches.
- Status: faceup
- First appeared: Round 3 - recruited at Narrow Path.
- Ability: Whenever a player attacks you, they **must** declare targets that add an even total of dice to your defense pool
- Ability cost: None (automatic)

## Daz, the Bandit Witch

- Source card: Dazzle
- Quirk: the first Bandit Chief to swear loyalty to the Empire. Speaks as if she's lived a thousand lives.
- Status: Denizen at Narrow Pass
- Ability: When played, discard all Hearth and Order cards at sites in your region.
- Ability cost: None (Spent when played. Will not trigger anymore.)
