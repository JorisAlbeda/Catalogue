# Converse — Lilianne & Naiad (Round 0, #1)

**Setting:** The Wooden Palace, Deep Woods. Lilianne receives Naiad in
audience and shows her the Verdflayme orb granted by the Old Oak.

**Lilianne:** "Naiad, why would the Forest grant me this? I have been
working so hard to preserve peace these months. Why was I given this
instrument of war? And why a Fire Talker's weapon, of all things?"

**Naiad:** "Fire Talkers. There hasn't been a Fire Talker in the Cradle
since before your grandfather's grandfather held that throne. They
didn't fight wars, Lily. They ended arguments — by burning everything
that could still have one. The Old Oak doesn't hand out gifts. He hands
out choices dressed as gifts. Whoever is testing you — they're not
going to stop because you're patient. What are you going to do with
it?"

**Lilianne:** "I must leave. The sign is clear, and I must obey it. We
will bring the orb with us. I will travel to the Drowned City, finally
see it for myself, and investigate it. But I would have your guidance,
Naiad. Would you travel with me?"

**Naiad:** *(a flinch, quickly hidden)* "Of course I'll come. You don't
ride anywhere without the Rangers, let alone there. But Lily — the
Drowned City. That ground hasn't been walked in eighteen years for a
reason. Let me send Rangers ahead first — not to stop you, to know what
we're walking into before you're standing in the middle of it. There
are things about that place I haven't told you. I was going to. Just —
not like this. Not with the Forest deciding the timing for me."

**Lilianne:** "Then it is decided. Send a scouting party ahead. I take
three warbands with me. Let them see a proper Chancellor travelling."

**Naiad:** "Three warbands. Good — enough to make them think twice, not
so many it looks like an invasion of your own history. I'll take First
Ranger Fenn ahead within the hour. If the Drowned City still has teeth,
I'd rather they bite scouts than my Chancellor. Lily — whatever you
find there, about the place, about the war, about your father — ask me
before you decide what it means. I was there. I know which parts of
that ruin lie. I'll have the warbands ready by dawn."

**[Conversation concluded]**
